Colorify

Do you want to create dramatic images by removing its colors and then recoloring it? Colorify app allows you to convert your pictures to black&white and then color any part of it by touching to the screen.

Take AWESOME pictures, recolor them, and SHARE with your friends!

+ Use your phone's camera or choose a picture from gallery.
+ Share them on Facebook wall and Twitter very easily!
+ Zoom to images with double fingers or double tapping.
+ Adjust brush size.
+ Undo your last actions.
+ Create fantastic pictures, have fun!!
